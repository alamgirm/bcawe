About BCAWE

BCAWE is a not-for-profit organization comprising of members from Bangladeshi community living in greater Windsor Essex. History of BCAWE began in 2004 when a group of like minded Bangladeshi felt to create a group to help each other.



If you are a former member of the executive committee or someone with futher knowledge about the history of BCAWE please contact bcawe@yahoo.ca so that we could update this page with true information.
