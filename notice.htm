Notice

Ekhushe February/International Mother Language Day 2015

February 17, 2015

BCAWE is announcing it's plan for the International Mother Language Day 2015 program. It will be held sometime in Febuary weather permitting. If the cold weather persists, we will have to delay it until the temperature improves. Details will be posted in due time.

Boishakhi Mela 1422

February 17, 2015

BCAWE is pleased to announce its plan for the upcoming Boishakhi festival. Like every year, we are requesting our members to have stalls, have the kids participate in arts and craft competition, and join the cultural program. The tentative date for Boishakhi Mela is 19th or 26th of April 2015. Timing of events and contact information will disseminated shortly.

Annual Picnic

October 11, 2014

Due to a fleeting Summer BCAWE is regretting that this year we will not have any picnic. Hopefully next year we will have a good Summer and a double sized picnic.

Iftar Potluck

July 1, 2014

Like every year, BCAWE is going to organize Iftar potluck on July 19th, Saturday at WIA Center on Dominion. We hope everyone would join us and we all would pray together to the Almighty for mercy.
