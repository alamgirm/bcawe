Yet Another Start

 

BCAWE is delighted to launch it new website. As you might have noticed the new site has its own Canadian domain. The decision to have bcawe.ca domain was taken by the executive members after lengthy discussions. BCAWE aspires to have a long lasting and wider impression of its presence. This new domain and website are the initial steps in that direction.

There are some other plans we have in mind. We'll communicate with you when the time comes. In the meantime please subscribe to the mailing list to get recent updates.

We are gradually adding more contents to this site. If you have some old photos and/or a story about BCAWE to share, please contact bcawe@yahoo.ca. We appreciate your contributions.
